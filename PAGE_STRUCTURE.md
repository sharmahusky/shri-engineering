## Content Strategy

### Static vs Dynamic Content

**Static Content:**
- Company information
- Product specifications (can be updated periodically)
- Service descriptions
- Client logos
- Certification information
- Contact information

**Dynamic/Reusable Content:**
- Product images (organized in `/public/assets/images/products/`)
- Specification tables (component-based)
- Inquiry forms (reusable component)
- Product cards (reusable component)

### Content Management Approach

1. **Constants Files** (`/src/lib/constants/`)
   - Products data with specifications
   - Services data
   - Clients data
   - Navigation items

2. **Type Definitions** (`/src/types/`)
   - TypeScript interfaces for type safety
   - Ensures consistency across components

3. **Component-Based Content**
   - Reusable components for specifications
   - Consistent formatting across pages
